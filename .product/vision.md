# Openpot — Product Vision

> **The "What and Who".** This document defines the business goals, product identity, and core constraints for this specific deployment.

---

## 1. Product Identity

| Field | Definition |
|---|---|
| **App Name** | Openpot |
| **Domain Area** | Cannabis (logging, social, medical) |
| **Vibe** | Organic, botanical, discreet, premium |

---

## 2. Target Audience

| Persona | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Primary** | The global cannabis community |
| **Use cases** | Journaling, strain tracking, local community discussion |

---

## 3. Top-Level Constraints

| Constraint | Rationale |
|---|---|
| **German BDSG Compliance** | Required to be legally robust in the German jurisdiction. |
| **"Stuttgart-Safe"** | Slang for zero-knowledge privacy. Even if the user's device is seized or our servers are compromised, user identities and actions must remain completely undecryptable and un-linkable to physical persons. |
